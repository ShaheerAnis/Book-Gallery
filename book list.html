Arthur : MIRZA GHALIB

About Aurthor

Mirza Ghalib was a famous Urdu and Persian poet of the Indian subcontinent in the 19th century. His original name was
Mirza Asadullah Baig Khan. His original pen-name was Asad but he changed it to Ghalib, which means 'conqueror'. According to
historical documents, Ghalib had no formal education.
